About Me

Hi! My name is Gustavo Luchetti, a 23 year-old full-stack developer currently based in São Paulo, Brazil. Although vague, I'd say 'liking computers' is my main hobby. I'm usually near one, and I've been interested in them for various reasons over the years: as a kid, for gaming on the cheap, as a teenager, I managed to build my own PC, and these days, for all sorts of programming related subjects.

Things I like

Web development (for work), artificial intelligence/data science, code automation (infrastructure as code, CI/CD, etc). and very recently, a bit of networking (configured my own homelab!).

I became very fond of A.I. in college, which led to my pursuit of an A.I. masters degree in the Netherlands, at Utrecht University. Although I had to pause my studies, I ended up working at Source.Ag, a fantastic A.I. startup that focused on increasing productivity for growers. With productivity in mind, I got quite deep in the Linux rabbit hole, from researching different window managers to tinkering my Neovim configuration file daily. I've mostly recovered, but I keep looking into ways to speed up my workflow.

Purpose

I see this website as both journal and portfolio. It’s very easy these days to consume content (or code) and to never give back, so this is my way of remeding that. My current goal is to write a blog post once a month, at the very least. I’ll try my best to stick to that schedule.
