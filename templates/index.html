Who are you?

Hi! My name is Gustavo Luchetti, a 23 year-old full-stack developer currently based in São Paulo, Brazil. I'm very much a 'computer guy'. I've been interested in them for various reasons over the years: as a kid, for gaming on the cheap, as a teenager, I managed to build my own PC, and these days, for all sorts of programming related subjects.

What subjects?

Web development (for work), artificial intelligence/data science, and all types of automation (infrastructure as code, CI/CD, etc). We all work enough, so let's focus on the latter two.

I became very fond of A.I. in college, which led to my pursuit of an A.I. masters degree in the Netherlands, at Utrecht University. Although I had to pause my studies, I ended up working at Source.Ag, a fantastic A.I. startup that focused on increasing productivity for growers. With productivity in mind, I got quite deep in the Linux rabbit hole, from researching different window managers to tinkering my Neovim configuration file daily. I've mostly recovered, but I keep looking into ways to speed up my workflow.

Why do this?

I see this website as both a journal and as a portfolio. It’s very easy these days to consume content (or code) and to never give back, so this is my way of remeding that. My current goal is to write a blog post once a month, at the very least. I’ll try my best to stick to that schedule.
